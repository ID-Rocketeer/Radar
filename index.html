1. RANGE RINGS

The scope displays 6 concentric green dashed range rings centered on the home station. The ring distances are scaled to the current range:

  • Ring 1 (10%): -- NM
  • Ring 2 (20%): -- NM
  • Ring 3 (40%): -- NM
  • Ring 4 (60%): -- NM
  • Ring 5 (80%): -- NM
  • Ring 6 (100%): -- NM (Scope boundary)

2. URL PARAMETERS

Modify query parameters in the address bar to configure the station location and radar range:

?lat=[latitude]&lon=[longitude]&rng=[range_nm]
  • Latitude options: lat, latitude (e.g. 30.1945)
  • Longitude options: lon, lng, long, longitude (e.g. -97.6699)
  • Range options: rng, range (clamped 2 to 250 NM)

Presets (Click to load):

  • Austin-Bergstrom (AUS) // 25 NM
  • Reagan Washington (DCA) // 10 NM
  • Nellis AFB (LSV) // 40 NM

3. SYSTEM FEATURES

  • TRAILS Toggle: Show or hide historical breadcrumbs.
  • FULLSCREEN Toggle: Expand scope view to fit screen.
  • TARGET FILTERS: Switch scope display between All traffic, Military only, Commercial, or General Aviation.
  • SELECT LOCATION: Recalibrate the radar home station coordinates and range. Click the button to enter calibration mode, which enables the LATITUDE, LONGITUDE, and RANGE fields for manual text input, and unlocks map panning and zooming. Drag the map to align the desired home location under the center crosshair reticle, zoom the map to adjust the visual range boundary, or type values directly into the active input fields, then click CONFIRM to apply or CANCEL to abort.
